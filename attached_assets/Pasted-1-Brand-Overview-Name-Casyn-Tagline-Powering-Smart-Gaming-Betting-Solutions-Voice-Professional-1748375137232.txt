1. Brand Overview
Name: Casyn
Tagline: Powering Smart Gaming & Betting Solutions
Voice: Professional, discreet, modern-tech
Positioning: Trusted partner in casino tech — software, kiosks, and affiliate infrastructure for underground networks and private operators.

2. Logo Usage
✅ Primary Logo
Full logo: Icon + “Casyn” wordmark

Preferred usage on light or white backgrounds

✅ Icon-Only Logo
Hexagonal C with embedded S can be used as standalone favicon, app icon, or stamp

Retain blue gradient and white negative space for clarity

❌ Do Not:
Alter proportions between icon and wordmark

Rotate or distort the hexagon icon

Change the colors without approval

Place on backgrounds with low contrast (e.g., dark blue on black)

3. Color Palette
Name	Hex	Usage
Casyn Blue	#1736F5	Primary accent, buttons, CTAs, links
Gradient Blue	#00A4FF → #0047FF	Logo highlight and icon depth
Jet Black	#000000	Text, logo wordmark, footer background
Graphite Gray	#808080	Secondary labels, inactive states
Soft Gray	#DEDEDE	Background fills, borders, forms
Highlight Yellow	#FFC709	Optional CTA highlight, alerts

4. Typography
Primary Font: Montserrat

Use for all headings and product names

Weights: SemiBold for headers, Regular for subheaders

Secondary Font: Inter

Use for body text, captions, UI copy

Sizes:

H1: 36–48px

H2: 28–32px

Body: 16–18px

Button: 14px UPPERCASE

5. Brand Voice & Tone
Tone Traits:

Trustworthy (for underground, high-risk markets)

Modern (tech-driven layout & voice)

Discreet (no loud marketing language)

Strategic (smart partner positioning)

Do Say:

“Casyn is the #1-ranked distributor for CMD & ProSys.”

“Built to simplify and protect casino operations.”

“Run smarter. Grow faster. Stay invisible.”

Avoid Saying:

“We’re the cheapest!”

“The ultimate public solution for everyone.”

Anything legally ambiguous in tone.

6. Logo Spacing & Sizing
Clear Space: Minimum padding = 1x the height of the “C” icon

Minimum Size:

Icon alone: 24px

Full logo: 120px width

7. Imagery Style
Photographic Style:

Dark-mode UIs and dashboards

Abstract tech textures (digital grids, circuits, networks)

Casino environments with soft blur/focus on people

Minimalist workspace setups or kiosk environments

Iconography Style:

Monoline or outline icons in white or electric blue

Consistent stroke weight

Avoid 3D or cartoon styles
